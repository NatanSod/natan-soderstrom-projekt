About the author:

I, the writer, am a teenage boy, and that is all I'm comfortable saying about that. I wrote this story for an assignment in web development. While the story isn't getting me a grade, the idea of telling this story is what truly motivates me, so I hope you'll give it a chance.

About the story:

"Wellcome to: Wadalond" is a choose-your-own-adventure webcomic about a person who leads quite a booring life escaping the struggle of the real world by creating more interesting worlds and exploring them in their very vivid dreams. Though maybe the dreams are a bit too vivid . You, the reader, will, at certain points in the story, be able to make some choices that will change the course of the story. Don't worry about the choices too much, you can always go back.

Things that are good to know.

The bookmark feature: Once you start reading you will see two buttons at the bottom of the page. One that says "Save Page" and one that says "Load Page". These are used to create a sort of bookmark. Pressing "Save Page" will make the website save which page you're currently on, and pressing "Load Page" will take you to the saved page if you have one.
