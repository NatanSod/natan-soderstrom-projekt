I can see it now; vast fields of green, a farm here, a tiny village there. A place with no worries, where you can be content simply watching the world go by. A great introduction to this world and a fantastic spot to start an adventure. A bit standard, but it’s a standard for a reason.

Now I just need to decide what role I will play.

Ah yes, the harsh, unforgiving cold of the winter waste land. Where few things grow and the only people that go there are researchers looking for the secrets of this untamed land of frost.

A good choice, but what position shall I take in this story?

Warm, huh… would have a volcano or two, be pretty close to the sea and… I know! A kingdom that puts a lot of value in physical strength and tradition. Where the highest ranking members of society, except for the rulers, are soldiers. There’s probably a lot of civil unrest, and some corruption going on behind the scenes. This is going to get interesting!

But should I be a pawn or a player in this game of politics?

The vast sea of yellow sand that goes as far as the eye can see, where the hot sun burns anything it touches, making water and shade a rare privilege. Many travel this harsh land in search of adventure and coin, and some find refuge near rivers; the cradles of civilization.

But who am I, traveling this harsh land?

The kingdom of darkness, the home of the wicked and the dangerous. A place where death and treasure is around every corner, and at the heart of it all is the black fortress, home to the dark ruler and source of the shadow creeping across the land. Feels kind of like skipping straight to the final act, but it will be an experience like no other.

Now I just need to decide through whose eyes I will see it from.

It’s important to stay humble, and being a part of this world is going to make me feel special enough just by itself. Who needs to save the world when you can have a nice life running a tavern, just talking to the residents and listening to the stories of travelers.

But who shall I be more specifically? That tavern idea seemed nice, let’s go with that!

Of course, “The Hero of Legend, the warrior chosen to defeat the darkness and bring light back to the land.” A tale as old as time, but one I am happy to take part in. I’ll meet and help many people, and at the end I’ll probably fight a god. It’s going to be great!

But let’s throw in a little twist; every great hero needs a weakness.

I don’t need to be important to have a good time. But why would a “normal” person be here? Maybe there’s a magic school, where I would be a student.

School might be boring, but magic makes everything more interesting, and maybe there’s some dark secret in the school... or maybe not, let’s make it a surprise!

Oh, this is going to be fun! The grand wizard and head of a research team at the forefront of their field, hidden away at the pole so that they won’t be “disturbed”.

I wonder how I will further science and what dark secrets I might discover.

Even an average life would be pretty interesting. But what would an average life be? Probably that of a soldier, serving their country, blind to the cracks in the facade of their homeland.

Maybe the bloodshed will open their eyes, but what would they do once they see the world with these new eyes? Well, not going to get anywhere by thinking about it.

Being important in this place is going to be fun! But who? The ruler, the main person in charge, is kind of booring. What else… hmmm. There’s probably a rebellion going on, being the leader of that would be way cooler!

A leader who has gained the respect of their peers and risen in the ranks, fighting for their fellow people, often getting by on a thin thread, because if nothing is risked, nothing will be gained.

This is going to be exciting!

I’m thinking something like a trader, traveling this land with my caravan, searching for wares and coin.

Maybe we’ll run into some peculiar customers, maybe we’ll run into some peculiar goods, maybe nothing will happen at all. In any case, I’m sure I’ll have some fun with it. Haggling always did seem kind of fun.

Like an explorer, searching for fame, glory, and of course, a bit of money wouldn’t hurt. Adventure is around every corner, I’ll just have to look, and I’ll meet a lot of people to help me or lead me astray, and maybe a tempel, filled with great treasure and deadly traps.

But even if I find nothing, I’m sure I’ll have some fun, after all, the search for adventure is an adventure in itself, is it not?

So, a minion? Just, a regular minion? Like a goblin or something? No, that’s boring, they barely even think for themselves. What about… oh? Yeah, yeah that could work. This will be…

What important people are there in the kingdom of darkness? There is… The dark ruler. Being evil isn’t something I enjoy, but every point of view is important, and maybe they’re not as evil as people believe.

“The road to hell is paved with good intentions”, I wonder which will pave mine.
