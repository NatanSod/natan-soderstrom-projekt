I can see it now; vast fields of green, a farm here, a tiny village there. A place with no worries, where you can be content simply watching the world go by. A great introduction to this world and a fantastic spot to start an adventure. A bit standard, but it’s a standard for a reason.

Now I just need to decide what role I will play.

Ah yes, the harsh, unforgiving cold of the winter waste land. Where few things grow and the only people that go there are researchers looking for the secrets of this untamed land of frost.

A good choice, but what position shall I take in this story?

Warm, huh… would have a volcano or two, be pretty close to the sea and… I know! A kingdom that puts a lot of value in physical strength and tradition. Where the highest ranking members of society, except for the rulers, are soldiers. There’s probably a lot of civil unrest, and some corruption going on behind the scenes. This is going to get interesting!

But should I be a pawn or a player in this game of politics?

The vast sea of yellow sand that goes as far as the eye can see, where the hot sun burns anything it touches, making water and shade a rare privilege. Many travel this harsh land in search of adventure and coin, and some find refuge near rivers; the cradles of civilization.

But who am I, traveling this harsh land?

The kingdom of darkness, the home of the wicked and the dangerous. A place where death and treasure is around every corner, and at the heart of it all is the black fortress, home to the dark ruler and source of the shadow creeping across the land. Feels kind of like skipping straight to the final act, but it will be an experience like no other.

Now I just need to decide through whose eyes I will see it from.
